This SOP alone is insufficient once a dispute has formally escalated.

3. Risk Areas Addressed
	•	Missed or misunderstood deadlines
	•	Verbal-only agreements
	•	Conflicting instructions to escrow holders
	•	Incomplete termination documentation

⸻

4. Core SOP

Phase 1: Contract Intake & Setup
	1.	Identify earnest money amount, holder, and delivery deadline as stated in the contract
	2.	Record all relevant dates in a written timeline tracker
	3.	Confirm deposit delivery method and acknowledgment from holder

Phase 2: Monitoring & Communication

5. Confirm material changes or issues via written communication

Phase 3: Termination or Completion

8. Route all release or disposition instructions in writing
9. Retain confirmation from escrow holder of received instructions
